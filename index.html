Our Story

PageTurn was born from a simple observation: India has 1.4 billion potential readers but access to quality books remains limited by cost, availability, and language barriers. We set out to change that.

Founded in Bengaluru in 2024, PageTurn offers a Netflix-style subscription to 50,000+ titles across fiction, non-fiction, business, science, and Indian literature — with offline reading, multi-language support, and community features that make reading social again.

Our Mission

To make every book accessible to every Indian reader — at a price that makes sense, in the language they love, on the device they already own.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025 · Last Updated: 1 June 2025

1. Information We Collect

PageTurn Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("PageTurn", "we", "us") collects the following categories of personal data when you use our platform:

  • Account data: name, email address, profile photo, reading preferences
  • Transaction data: purchase history, payment method (tokenised — we never store raw card numbers), billing address
  • Usage data: books read, reading progress, session duration, device type, IP address
  • Communication data: support tickets, reviews, community requests

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve our services, personalise your reading recommendations, process payments, send transactional emails, and comply with legal obligations under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 (India).

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with: payment processors (Razorpay), cloud infrastructure providers (AWS), analytics partners (anonymised), and as required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your subscription plus 2 years. Transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian accounting law.

5. Your Rights (DPDP Act)

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to nominate a data guardian
  • Right to grievance redressal

Exercise your rights at: privacy@pageturn.in

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and optional analytics cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. Contact

Data Protection Officer: dpo@pageturn.in
Grievance Officer: grievance@pageturn.in

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 1 June 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit PageTurn. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function. These cannot be disabled. They include: authentication tokens, session management, security cookies.

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences such as reading theme (dark/light), font size, and language. Can be disabled in settings.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with PageTurn (pages visited, time spent, features used). Data is anonymised. Uses: Google Analytics, Mixpanel.

Marketing Cookies

Used to show relevant book recommendations on partner platforms. Requires explicit consent.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our Cookie Preferences panel (accessible from the footer). Disabling analytics/marketing cookies will not affect your ability to use PageTurn.
